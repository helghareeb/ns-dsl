The editor's only requested change is the **Data Availability** declaration: the previous statement's
"available from the corresponding author on reasonable request" and "released … upon acceptance" are
not acceptable under Scientific Reports' open-data policy, and the declaration in the SNAPP system must
match the manuscript exactly.

## What was changed in this revision

   "on reasonable request / upon acceptance": the figure/table-underlying summaries now ship as
   **Supplementary Information**, and the raw data + code are **archived in Zenodo** with reviewer
   access during review and open release on publication.
2. The aggregated result summaries are now attached as Supplementary Information data
   (`Supplementary_Data_1.zip`, 14 CSVs) and documented in the SI (new "Supplementary Data" section).
3. Two ready-to-upload Zenodo packages prepared (see below).
