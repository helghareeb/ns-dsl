"available from the corresponding author on reasonable request" and "released … upon acceptance" are

## What was changed in this revision
1. **Data Availability** and **Code Availability** statements rewritten (manuscript + README) to remove
   "on reasonable request / upon acceptance".
2. The figure/table-underlying summaries now ship as **Supplementary Information**
   (`Supplementary_Data_1.zip`, 14 CSVs; new "Supplementary Data" section in the SI).
3. The raw data **and** the full code are archived together in **one restricted Zenodo record** (a
   single DOI), with reviewer access via a private link and open release on publication.
